Daily History is a momentary blast to the past, and a minimalist alternative for your browser startpage or homepage. It centers around today's date, presenting one fact at a time about that date in history. It's a simple way to appreciate the significance of each day, or even just to declutter your virtual space when you open your browser.

Open your browser's settings and enter https://daily-history.netlify.app as your startpage or homepage to get your daily dose of history.
